Here at the UCLA Library we want to share with you one of our favorite time-saving tools. Zotero is a great (and free!) software program that helps you collect and organize your sources. It allows you to create bibliographies in virtually any citation style with just the click of a button.

We have been working on developing a tutorial to help students get started with Zotero. We would love for you to give it a try and would greatly appreciate your feedback.
